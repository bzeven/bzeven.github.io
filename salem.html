About Salem

Welcome to Salem, Oregon's charming capital city, where history meets modern allure amidst the scenic Willamette Valley. Explore the iconic Oregon State Capitol, crowned with a golden pioneer statue, and discover our rich heritage at the Willamette Heritage Center.

Art lovers can indulge in the Hallie Museum of Art's diverse collections, from contemporary masterpieces to Native American treasures. Stroll along tree-lined streets, relax in picturesque parks, and immerse yourself in Salem's vibrant downtown, bustling with local eateries, craft breweries, and unique boutiques. Whether you're here for history, culture, or simply to unwind, Salem promises a memorable experience at every turn.
