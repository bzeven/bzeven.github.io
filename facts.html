Oregon Fun Facts

  1. Navy blue and gold are Oregon’s official state colors.
  2. The “Oregon Pioneer” statue that tops the capitol building is a work by Ulric Ellerhusen. This heroic figure represents the spirit of Oregon’s early settlers.
  3. Oregon is the birthplace of The Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
  4. Tater tots were invented in Oregon.
  5. Oregon has the most ghost towns of any state.
  6. Portland’s name was chosen in a coin flip.
  7. At 8,000 feet deep Hells Canyon is the deepest river gorge in North America.
  8. Springfield Oregon is the real life inspiration for the Simpson’s hometown.
  9. The official state motto of Oregon is ‘Alis volat propriis’ which translates to ‘She flies with her own wings’.
  10. The origin of the state's name is a mystery.
